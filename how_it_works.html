How EasyBid Works

1

Browse Auctions

Explore our wide range of items — from antiques to electronics — and choose what excites you the most.

2

Place Your Bid

Click on Bid Now to enter your offer. Make sure it’s higher than the current bid to stay ahead.

3

Real-Time Updates

Stay updated with instant notifications on the latest bids, so you never miss a chance to win.

4

Winning the Auction

When the auction ends, the highest bidder wins! You’ll receive a confirmation email with payment details.

5

Secure Payment & Delivery

Make a secure payment and have your item delivered to your doorstep with full buyer protection.
